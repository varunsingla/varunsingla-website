01 — Who I Am

I'm Varun Singla, a Director at Deloitte Digital based in Singapore, working within the Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT) practice. My work sits at the intersection of enterprise AI strategy, agentic AI architecture, Salesforce platform implementation, and digital transformation for telcos.

I started this daily research journal in March 2026 to document my deep dives into the rapidly evolving agentic AI landscape — covering everything from LLM token economics to multi-agent orchestration frameworks, agent memory systems, governance frameworks, and production deployment patterns.

With a background in telecommunications infrastructure (OSS/BSS), contact center platforms, and Salesforce Communications Cloud, I bring an enterprise practitioner's lens to AI research — focused on what actually works at scale, not just what's theoretically interesting.
